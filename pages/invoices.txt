====== Invoices ======
Quickly and easily invoice customers & track payments

{{invoices.png?direct|}}

===== Invoice Fields =====

  * Client
  * Date
  * Category
  * Scope
  * Item/Product
  * Description
  * Quantity
  * Price
  * Tax
  * Notes

===== Features =====
  * Recurring invoices
  * Email invoice details to client
  * Client can review invoices in portal or using a guest link
{{:invoice-guest_url.png?direct|}}
  * Duplicate/copy invoices
  * Track payment of invoices
  * Stripe invoice payments - one off and recurring against saved payment method
