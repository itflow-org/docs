====== Quotes ======
Quickly generate & send quotes to your clients.

{{quotes.png|}}

===== Quote Fields =====
  * Client
  * Date
  * Category
  * Scope
  * Item/Product
  * Description
  * Quantity
  * Price
  * Tax
  * Notes

  * Email quote details to client
  * Client can approve/decline quote using a guest link

  * Duplicate/copy quotes
  * 1-click to convert accepted quote to invoice
